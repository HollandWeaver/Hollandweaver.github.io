Upscale restaurant / Lounge

Cocktails

Precision and knowledge. No recipe checking.

CosmopolitanMartiniOld FashionedManhattanNegroniMai TaiEspresso MartiniFrench 75

Wine

Beyond grape names. Understand major regions (Napa, Burgundy, Bordeaux, Rioja, Tuscany), winemaking basics — oak vs. stainless, malolactic fermentation, Old World vs. New World.

Cocktail bar / Fine dining

Cocktails

Codex framework matters most. Know root templates, history, and how to riff.

BoulevardierOaxacan Old FashionedLast WordSazeracVesper MartiniBee's KneesJungle BirdPaper PlaneCorpse Reviver #2Penicillin

Wine & spirits

Deep knowledge. Discuss terroir, vintage variation, producer styles. Know your amari, vermouths, and fortified wines.

Beer — expected at every venue type

Know ales (top-fermenting, warmer, fuller) vs. lagers (bottom-fermenting, colder, crisper).

Major styles

Pilsner (lager)Pale Ale (ale)IPA (ale)Porter (ale)Stout (ale)Wheat Beer (ale)Amber/Red AleLager/Mexican Lager

If the menu has local microbrewery beers, learn those specifically. Often the first thing a manager quizzes.
